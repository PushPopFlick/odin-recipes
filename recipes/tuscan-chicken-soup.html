Tuscan Chicken Soup

A bowl of comfort in only 30 minutes

This Tuscan chicken soup features features chicken breast in creamy broth with spinach and sun-dried tomatoes that is especially comforting on those dreary winter days.

Nutritional Facts

Calories: 374
Protien: 35g
Carbs: 17g
Fat: 19g

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 4 (8.25 ounce) containers chicken bone broth
  • 1 ½ teaspoons Tuscan seasoning***
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 2 handfuls roughly chopped spinach
  • 4 tablespoons sun dried tomatoes
  • 1 cup high protein pasta
  • 4 tablespoons finely grated Parmesan cheese
  • ½ cup heavy cream

Directions

Step 1

Gather all your ingredients and kitchenware.

Step 2

Add oil to a saucepan over medium heat. Once oil is hot, add onion, and sauté until translucent, about 5 minutes. Add garlic; saute until fragrant; about 1 minute.

Step 3

Stir in tomato paste until well combined. Add chicken bone broth, Tuscan seasoning, chicken pieces, sun-dried tomatoes, and torn spinach; stir to combine. Add pasta; simmer until pasta is tender with a bite, about 10 minutes.

Step 4

Sprinkle in Parmesan; stir to combine. Stir in heavy cream.

Serve immediately and ENJOY!
